## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

Harbour Tiling ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage our services.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

**Contact Information:**
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Suburb or location

**Project Information:**
- Details about your tiling project
- Property type and access requirements
- Photos or descriptions of the work area

**Communication Records:**
- Emails, messages, and phone call notes
- Form submissions through our website

---

### 2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

- **Contact forms** on our website
- **Email correspondence**
- **Phone conversations**
- **Site inspections and consultations**
- **Website analytics** (see Cookies section below)

---

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

- Respond to your enquiries and provide quotes
- Schedule and complete tiling work
- Communicate about your project
- Send project-related updates and reminders
- Improve our services and website
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

---

### 4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

- **Service providers** who assist with our business operations (e.g., email services, accounting)
- **Subcontractors** if they are involved in your project (with your knowledge)
- **Legal authorities** if required by law or to protect our rights

All third parties are required to handle your information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

---

### 5. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Improve website functionality
- Analyse website traffic and usage patterns
- Remember your preferences

**Types of cookies we may use:**

- **Essential cookies:** Required for the website to function properly
- **Analytics cookies:** Help us understand how visitors use our site

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

---

### 6. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services including:

- **Web3Forms:** For processing contact form submissions
- **Netlify:** For website hosting
- **Google Fonts:** For typography

These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

---

### 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Secure HTTPS connection on our website
- Limited access to personal information
- Secure storage of digital records

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

### 8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide our services
- Maintain records for warranty and legal purposes
- Comply with legal obligations

Contact and project information is typically retained for 7 years after project completion for warranty and record-keeping purposes.

---

### 9. Your Rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Request deletion** of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Withdraw consent** for marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

---

### 10. Marketing Communications

We may send you project-related communications, but we do not send unsolicited marketing emails. If you receive any communications from us that you wish to stop, please contact us and we will promptly remove you from our mailing list.

---

### 11. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

### 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
