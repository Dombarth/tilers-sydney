At Harbour Tiling we specialise in the aspects of bathroom tiling, kitchen tiling, floor tiling and waterproofing for residential and commercial customers across the Sydney areas. The team have been providing tiling services for over fifteen years and are fully licensed to carry out all aspects of wall and floor tiling work.

We service customers throughout the Sydney region including Ryde, West Ryde, North Ryde, Eastwood, Epping, Gladesville and surrounding suburbs areas. Harbour Tiling are fully insured and all work carried out comes with a guarantee on workmanship. As such we are able to offer customers peace of mind when it comes to the quality of tiling work.

The tilers at Harbour Tiling pride themselves on providing the highest form of quality workmanship while still remaining competitive on pricing. Whether its a small bathroom renovation or a large commercial fitout, we treat every job with the same level of care and attention to detail.

Our services include bathroom tiling, kitchen splashback tiling, wall and floor tiling, outdoor tiling, balcony tiling and waterproofing coordination. We work with ceramic tiles, porcelain tiles, natural stone and large format tiles depending on what is required.

For bathroom tiling projects we can coordinate waterproofing which is completed to Australian standards. The team have experience with all types of substrate preparation including concrete, timber floors and removal of existing tiles.

At Harbour Tiling we take pride in providing quality tiling services while still maintaining honest and upfront pricing.
