Why tiling work matters

Good tiling lasts for years. It's one of the few finishes in a home that people see and touch every day. When it's done well, it looks effortless. When it's not, you notice.

Most tiling problems come from what happens before the tiles go on — preparation, substrate condition, waterproofing sequencing, and layout planning. That's where I focus.

How projects work

  • Initial conversation You share what you're planning. I ask questions, explain what's involved, and let you know if it's something I can help with.
  • Site assessment I look at the space in person — substrate condition, access, sequencing with other trades. This informs an accurate quote.
  • Clear quote and scope You receive a written quote with clear inclusions. No hidden costs. If something's not included, I'll explain why.
  • Preparation and tiling Work begins with proper prep — levelling, priming, waterproofing coordination. Then tiling, grouting, and finishing.
  • Handover Final walkthrough, care instructions, and answers to any questions. I don't disappear once the job is done.
